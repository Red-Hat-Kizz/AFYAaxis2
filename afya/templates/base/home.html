Frequently asked questions

What are the symptoms of the flu?

The flu typically presents with fever, cough, sore throat, body aches, and fatigue.

How can I prevent the spread of COVID-19?

Wearing masks, practicing social distancing, and frequent hand washing are effective measures.

What is the recommended vaccination schedule for children?

Children should receive vaccinations according to the CDC's recommended schedule.

What should I do if I have a severe allergic reaction?

Seek immediate medical attention and use an epinephrine auto-injector if available.

How often should I get a health check-up?

Adults should have a health check-up at least once a year, or as recommended by their doctor.

What are the benefits of regular exercise?

Regular exercise improves cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, and enhances mental well-being.
